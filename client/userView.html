Welcome to iPlan!

iPlan was created to make event planning easier for everyone!

Whether it's a birthday party or a work-related outing, simply create a new event by giving it a name, location, date, and time - and you are ready to add some choices for places to go and invite your friends.

When your guests visit your event page, they can vote for the places you selected or add their own suggestions until there is a clear winner, or the voting timer runs out (Did we mention you can create a timer for voting cutoff? You can create a timer for voting cutoff.)

There you have it! Easy decision-making, saved time, and happy people.
